# Contributing Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the BFSI Regulatory Analyzer! We appreciate your help in making this project better. Please follow the guidelines below when contributing:

## Contributing Compliance Documents
- **Format**: Make sure to use the correct format specified in the project documentation.
- **Validation**: Ensure all compliance documents are validated against current regulatory requirements.
- **Review**: Submit your contributions through pull requests for review.

## Regulatory Updates
- **Frequency**: Stay up-to-date on the latest regulations affecting BFSI.
- **Notification**: Inform the team of any significant changes to regulations promptly.
- **Documentation**: Update the related documentation to reflect new regulations.

## Knowledge Base Articles
- **Topics**: Articles should cover relevant topics that contribute to the community’s understanding of GRC practices.
- **Quality**: Ensure articles are well-researched, clear, and concise.
- **Citations**: Include citations for external resources where necessary.

## GRC Templates
- **Structure**: Follow the project’s template structure for consistency.
- **Customization**: Customize templates based on the specific needs of compliance frameworks.
- **Feedback**: Be open to feedback and revisions on your template contributions.

## Code of Conduct
- We expect all contributors to follow our [Code of Conduct](link_to_code_of_conduct) to maintain a respectful and inclusive environment.
- All interactions in the contribution process should remain professional and constructive.

## Thank You!
We appreciate every contribution, big or small. Together, we can build a comprehensive resource for BFSI regulatory practices!
